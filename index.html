About Us

Computer & Network Systems is based in Thames on the Coromandel Peninsula.

At Computer & Network Systems, we provide our business clients with flexible on-site and remote support.

Supporting Businesses for Over 20 Years

A functional and efficient IT system is an essential business tool. We have been supporting businesses for over 20 years.


Computer systems are not just about hardware. We understand the links between hardware, software and the internet. Often acting as the middleman to identify if the problem is hardware or software based and we happily work with our customers software vendors to resolve issues quickly.

We Sell All Things Hardware

From a mouse to high-end servers, we offer a wide range of hardware, from simple hard drive replacements to virtualized server installs.
